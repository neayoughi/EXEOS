Custom Tees is planning an online advertising campaign using A different ad types across two web companies.

The company aims to achieve:
- At least goal_young thousand clicks from users aged 18–25.
- At least goal_old thousand clicks from users older than 25.
- At least goal_unique_young thousand unique clicks from users aged 18–25.
- At least goal_unique_old thousand unique clicks from users older than 25.

Each ad type a has the following characteristics:
- Estimated percentage of clicks from 18–25-year-olds: young_clicks[a].
- Estimated percentage of clicks from users over 25: old_clicks[a].
- Estimated percentage of unique clicks: unique_clicks[a].
- Cost per 1000 clicks: costs[a].
- Maximum allowable number of clicks (in thousands): max_clicks[a].

The objective is to determine how many clicks to purchase from each ad type to minimize the total advertising cost, while meeting all click and unique click goals.
