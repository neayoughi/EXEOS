To operate a cafeteria efficiently, the manager must ensure adequate staffing based on daily staff requirements. The number of employees needed on day n is given by num_n. Each employee works n_working_days consecutively followed by n_resting_days off.

The objective is to calculate the minimum number of employees required to satisfy the staffing requirements.

The outputs include:
- total_number: the total number of employees to employ.
- is_work: a binary schedule indicating if each employee is working or resting on each day.
